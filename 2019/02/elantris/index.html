Elantris

Written by Shirley Zhou with ♥ on February 14, 2019 in Reading

Elantris was pretty freaking legit.

Thoughts dump

  • It’s clear that this is an early book, and Sanderson recycles themes.[^] The main characters here have clear echos with Mistborn – it’s as if Mistborn is an expanded version of Elantris. Arguably not as successful because it’s not as concise.
  • Sanderson books feature strong female characters. 1

Thoughts dump 2

[^]: I never quite understood why recycling themes is such a sin among writers, e.g. criticism of Brian Jacques. The similarity is comforting; the theme is what makes them so enthralling in the first place. But maybe I’m just too sentimental.

  1. A sticking point for me. ↩︎
